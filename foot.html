Football

Football is one of the most popular sports in the world and our university has a proud tradition in this sport. We have a men's team and a women's team that compete in national and international competitions. Our teams have won several championships over the years and we are always striving to achieve more.

Men's Team

Our men's football team is made up of some of the most talented players from around the world. They compete in the top division of the national league and have also played in several international competitions.

Women's Team

Our women's football team is equally talented and dedicated. They have also won several championships over the years and continue to be a force to be reckoned with in the national and international competitions.

Facilities

Our university has state-of-the-art facilities for football including a full-sized football pitch, a training ground, and a stadium that can seat up to 50,000 spectators.

Welcome to the International University Football Program

History

The International University football team was established in 1950 and has since become one of the most successful collegiate football programs in the country. The team has won 8 national championships and has produced numerous All-American players.
