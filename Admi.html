Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Official transcripts
  • Standardized test scores (e.g., SAT, ACT)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Application fee

Application Deadline

The application deadline for the fall semester is August 1, and the deadline for the spring semester is December 1. Late applications will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
