LUCT University Sports Tournament

The annual LUCT University Sports Tournament is a highly anticipated event, bringing together athletes from various schools and universities across the country to compete in a variety of sports. The tournament is held over the course of one week and features competitions in the following sports:

  • Football
  • Basketball
  • Volleyball
  • Tennis
  • Swimming

Each team is allowed to register up to 15 players for each sport, with a maximum of 3 sports per team. The tournament is open to both men and women, with separate competitions held for each category.

Interested participants can register for the tournament by filling out the online registration form available on the official tournament website. The registration fee is shs50,000 per team, per sport.

Tournament Rules

  1. Each game will consist of two 45-minute halves.
  2. If the game is tied after regulation time, it will go to a penalty shootout.
  3. Each team may have a maximum of 5 substitutions per game.
  4. Players receiving a red card will be suspended for the next game.
  5. Players receiving two yellow cards in different games will be suspended for the next game.
  6. All players must bring valid student ID for registration.
