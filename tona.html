Welcome to the Sports Tournament

The University Name Sports Tournament is an annual event that brings together students from different departments and faculties to compete in various sports. The tournament is organized by the Department of Sports and is open to all students who are interested in participating.

This year's tournament will be held from May 1st to May 15th. The event will feature the following sports:

  • Football
  • Basketball
  • Volleyball
  • Tennis
  • Table Tennis
  • Badminton

To register for the tournament, please visit the Department of Sports in person or contact us via phone or email. The deadline for registration is April 15th.

Football Tournament

The football tournament is one of the most popular events in the Sports Tournament. It is open to all students and is played in a knockout format. The rules of the tournament are as follows:

  • Each team can have a maximum of 11 players.
  • Matches will be played for 90 minutes, divided into two halves of 45 minutes each.
  • In the event of a draw, the match will go to penalties.

The winners of the football tournament will receive a trophy and a cash prize.

Basketball Tournament

The basketball tournament is another exciting event in the Sports Tournament. It is also open to all students and is played in a league format. The rules of the tournament are as follows:

  • Each team can have a maximum of 12 players.
  • Matches will be played for 40 minutes, divided into two halves of 20 minutes each.
  • In the event of a tie, the match will go to overtime.

The winners of the basketball tournament will receive a trophy and a cash prize.
